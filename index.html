Frequently asked questions

How do I change the breathing rhythm?

On the main screen, tap the Settings (gear) icon. Under Breathing Rhythm, pick a preset such as 4–6 Calm, 5–5 Balance, or Box Breathing.

Where are volume and haptic controls?

Audio and haptics live on the Settings screen. Tap the gear icon, then open Audio & Haptics to adjust the metronome or toggles.

Does the app work on Apple Watch?

Yes. BreatheWith includes a companion experience for Apple Watch that works with the iPhone app, using haptics to guide your breath on your wrist.

Sound or haptics stopped working — what should I try?

Confirm the device is not in Silent Mode (iPhone) or that media volume is up (Android). In the app, revisit Audio & Haptics and ensure features are enabled. On Watch, check that haptics are allowed in system settings.

Is there a subscription or data tracking?

No. BreatheWith is built for privacy: no analytics SDKs in the app as described in our policy, and your practice data stays on your device. Pricing (one-time purchase or free) depends on region and store listing.
