BreatheWith: help, downloads, and FAQs

Download

BreatheWith is a breathing pacer: you choose a rhythm—box, 4–6, balance, or another preset—and follow an on-screen visual guide together with audio and haptic cues, so you see the pattern, hear it, and feel it—without counting in your head. It runs on iPhone and Apple Watch (with iPhone) from the App Store, and on Android from Google Play—use the buttons below.

App StoreGoogle Play

If search does not show the app in your region, open the store on your device and search for BreatheWith by name.

Google Play closed testing

We run a closed test before a wide Play release—the same flow Google documents for production access (enough opted-in testers on the track for long enough). Use the same Google account everywhere: the one on your Android device’s Play Store, your tester list entry, and when you tap opt-in.

  1. Join the tester list. Enter the email for the Google account you use on Google Play (often Gmail, or another address if that is your Google account) in the form below and tap Send. After we accept your address and add it in Play Console, we email you with the opt-in link and what to tap next. You may also get a separate invite from Google Play—either way, you must complete opt-in. If your track uses a Google Group, join that group when your admin asks.
  2. Opt in on Google Play. Use the link in our email, or tap Opt in on Google Play below (same URL as the web test link), then confirm in Play. That step turns on access so you can install the test build at no charge as a tester. Skipping opt-in means you are not counted as opted-in.
  3. Install from Play. After opting in, install the app from the Play Store flow for testers.

Submissions are delivered by FormSubmit to our support inbox. After we accept your address and add it for testing, we email you with the opt-in link so you can complete the step—Google may send its own invite too. Once you’re opted in, you can install on the closed track for free as a tester.

Frequently asked questions

How do I change the breathing rhythm?

On the main screen, tap the Settings (gear) icon. Under Breathing Rhythm, pick a preset such as 4–6 Calm, 5–5 Balance, or Box Breathing.

Where are volume and haptic controls?

Audio and haptics live on the Settings screen. Tap the gear icon, then open Audio & Haptics to adjust the metronome or toggles.

Does the app work on Apple Watch?

Yes. BreatheWith includes a companion experience for Apple Watch that works with the iPhone app, using haptics to guide your breath on your wrist.

Sound or haptics stopped working — what should I try?

Confirm the device is not in Silent Mode (iPhone) or that media volume is up (Android). In the app, revisit Audio & Haptics and ensure features are enabled. On Watch, check that haptics are allowed in system settings.

Is there a subscription or data tracking?

No. BreatheWith is built for privacy: no analytics SDKs in the app as described in our policy, and your practice data stays on your device. Pricing (one-time purchase or free) depends on region and store listing.

How do I join the Android closed test on Google Play?

On Google Play closed testing, send your Google account email with the form. After it’s accepted, we email you with the opt-in link. Then install from Play—test installs are free for testers.
